Since our Rebirth, we've been moving towards something extraordinary.

We partnered with Makalot, a global garment manufacturing giant with a front-row commitment to compliance — because leaving the earth a little better than we found it matters to us.

我們與全球服裝製造巨擘 Makalot 聚陽實業合作，一間以合規為先的企業——因為讓地球變得更好一點，對我們來說很重要。

We chose Tencel and Umorfil for the same reason. Recycled from natural fibres and fish collagen. We're proud of that choice.

我們選擇 Tencel 和 Umorfil 也是同樣的原因。取自天然纖維與魚鱗膠原蛋白的再生材質，我們為這個選擇感到驕傲。

We built our own logistics — to store, organise, and prepare your garments with the care they deserve — and to make sure they arrive on time for the moments that matter.

我們打造了自己的物流——用心存放、整理、準備每件衣物——確保它們在重要時刻準時送達你手中。

我們在大安開設實體店，只為一個理由：給你空間和時間，不被打擾地試穿——沒有壓力，不用急。

We believe these garments should serve your body. Make you feel so good that your morning becomes effortless. Every piece carries our care for you — hoping you can glow and be ready for the world in just a few minutes.

我們相信這些衣物應該服務於你的身體。讓你感覺如此舒適，以至於早晨變得毫不費力。每一件都承載著我們對你的用心——希望你能在幾分鐘內，發光準備好迎接世界。
